About

From large medical and industrial facilities to small office tenant improvements, Airco Mechanical, Inc. provides cost and energy efficient mechanical systems throughout the Northern California. Our 100,000 square foot sheet metal and pipe fabrication facility is state-of-the-art and unparalleled in Northern California. We take a team approach to every project, with engineers, estimators, project managers and environmental controls specialists. Airco Mechanical was founded in 1974 by James C. Jones. Mr. Jones believed there was a better way to serve the industry. Mr. Jones established a full service engineering and construction company that would focus on design build projects. Airco has since grown to keep up with construction best practices by incorporating virtual design and construction (VDC), building automation management, (BIM) and most recently a service department.

Mr. Jones continued to lead the company well into his 80’s. In 2008 a new era began when Mr. Jones turned the company over to his son, Wyatt. Wyatt Jones became President and has been leading the company ever since. It was Wyatt’s vision to expand to Treasure Island and to bring a service department under the Airco brand.

Airco has a trusted team which is led by experienced professionals. We have many team members with 15, 20, and even 30+ years of service. The longevity and loyalty of our employees ensures we are learning from the past, teaching and training new employees in best practices, and installing new products as industry trends evolve.

Airco Mechanical is proud to support a number of non-profit organizations in our community. We actively support Contractors Caring for Kids annual Holiday Toy Drive, Salvation Army and the American Heart Association.
