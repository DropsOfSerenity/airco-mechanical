Services

Exceeding the needs of today’s construction industry has inspired the design/build philosophy at Airco Mechanical, Inc. Airco is a single source for the entire mechanical system of a building, from design to complete installation, to warranty and after in the preventative maintenance and service of the building.

Engineering

Our engineering department is a critical asset to the design / build process. We work with our clients from the first conception of their project to completion. We are staffed with experienced licensed mechanical engineers, designers and CAD Operators.

Airco’s engineering team has the ability to design comprehensive mechanical systems specific to our clients needs, always considering a project's budget, schedule and sustainability.

Airco Automation provides products and services intended to maximize comfort and efficiency within commercial buildings, institutional buildings, as well as data centers. Building automation systems optimize the performance and maintenance of multiple building control systems. Airco uses Automated Logic control products that enable building owners / managers to integrate, automate and manage their buildings easily and efficiently. Building automation systems greatly increase occupant comfort, decrease energy use and allow remote monitoring and control from anywhere in the world.

As a TABB certified* company, Airco Automation, Inc offers design professionals, engineers and building owners the highest standard in testing and balancing of commercial HVAC systems.

Airco’s Testing, Adjusting and Balancing (TAB) Services include:
  • TABB (Testing Adjusting and Balancing Bureau)
    • Certified TABB Reports
    • ANSI (American National Standards Institute)
    • TAB Services
      • Hydronic balance
      • Air Balance
      • Outside Air testing and documentation
  • Fire Life Safety (FLS)
    • Certified technicians and supervisors
    • Periodic Damper Inspection Program
    • Duct Smoke Detector Velocity Testing
  • Indoor Air Quality (IAQ)
    • Investigation and solution Services
  • Hospital, Laboratory and Clean room Services
    • Perform 2009 Clean room Certification to ISO 14644 Standards
  • Isolation Room Testing
    • HEPA Filter Integrity Tests
    • Particle Counting
    • Shaker Calibration
    • Walk-in freezer mapping
    • CDA Clean Dry Air Testing
    • Bacterial Counts
    • Fume Hood Certification
      • ASHRAE 110 Test
  • Sensor Calibration
    • Oxygen sensor
    • CO2 sensor
    • Humidity sensor
  • Specialty Adjustment and Testing Services
    • Laser Alignment of belts, sheaves, and motors
